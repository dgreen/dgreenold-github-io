Feed the Google: Ie7 Png Jpg MIME Confusion

Sep 27th, 2007 | Comments

So DavidB and I are working on a new bug in OUMgt –> folks can’t upload .jpg and .png files into our RAILS app. This used to work.

After a bit of exploring (much more for the PNG issue than the jpg issue, and actually the help of gracious Internet developer), we have found:

IE7 marks x.jpg with the Mime type of image/pjpeg and marks the file x.png with the Mime type of image/x-png .

Sigh.
